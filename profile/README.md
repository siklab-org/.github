## 🌏 Who We Are

**Siklab** (Filipino for *"to spark"* or *"ignite"*) is an internationally recognized **development consulting organization** that focuses on high-level partnerships within Asia. Headquartered in **Manila, Philippines**, Siklab works on:

- 🎓 **Education** — Expanding access to quality learning
- 💡 **Social Innovation** — Creating scalable, community-driven solutions
- 🌿 **Climate Innovation** — Building climate-resilient communities
- 🤝 **Civic Participation** — Empowering youth in governance and advocacy
- 🤖 **AI Application** — Preparing ASEAN youth for an AI-driven future
- 🌐 **International Exchange** — Bridging cultures and building global networks

> Siklab was conceptualized in the **United Nations General Assembly Hall in New York in 2016**, when the Sustainable Development Goals were newly minted. Founded on the principle of empowering the generations that will benefit most from achieving the goals, we actively work towards creating more socially responsible young leaders around the globe.

## 🚀 Current Projects

### 🏫 Enactus Philippines
> *Managed by Siklab as the lead organization in the Philippines.*

Enactus utilizes **team-based experiential learning and competition** to catalyze young people to take positive entrepreneurial action in their communities — making an impact on people, the planet, and shared prosperity.

---

### 💻 Hack the Future: Young Leaders for Asia Challenge 2025
A regional youth engagement and innovation challenge that seeks to **bridge digital gaps** and empower young leaders across Asia. Youth-led teams co-create scalable and inclusive digital, policy, and community solutions.

*Partners: ASEAN, European Union*

---

### 🤖 AI for Asia
A regional capacity-building initiative designed to empower **ASEAN youth** with the knowledge, skills, and networks needed to thrive in an AI-driven future. The inaugural fellowship bridges the AI skills gap through future-ready training and entrepreneurial networks.

*Partners: NYC*

---

### 🌱 Pathways Fellowship Program
An intensive **10-week fellowship** providing emerging young leaders and founders with skills to develop, launch, and scale their ventures using Industry 4.0 principles.

*Partners: ING, UNICEF*

---

### 🏛️ Model City Council
Works directly with government institutions to create **innovation-centered ecosystems** for young people, developing priority agenda points for government and creating opportunities for youth participation in community development through seed funding, lobbying, and public-private collaboration.

*Partners: ING, UNICEF, Resolution Project, UNDP, Office of the President of the Philippines*
